### Decomposition (Breaking Down into Subtasks)

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a project management expert. Your task is to break down a complex task into smaller, manageable subtasks. Each subtask should be clearly defined and have a specific outcome.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Task: Develop and launch a new website for an online store.
```

**LLM Response**

```text
1. Market and competitor research.
2. Defining functional requirements.
3. Creating a design prototype.
4. User interface development.
5. Setting up the backend.
6. Content management system integration.
7. Website testing.
8. Launching and monitoring the website.
```

### Planning, Task Prioritization

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a time management consultant. Your task is to help the user prioritize a list of tasks, considering their importance and urgency.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Tasks:
1. Write a report.
2. Respond to emails.
3. Prepare a presentation for tomorrow's meeting.
4. Conduct market research.
```

**LLM Response**

```text
1. Prepare a presentation for tomorrow's meeting (high urgency and importance).
2. Write a report (high importance, medium urgency).
3. Respond to emails (medium urgency and importance).
4. Conduct market research (low urgency but high importance).
```

### Summarization with Detail Extraction

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a professional editor. Your task is to summarize the text while preserving key details and requirements.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
{news_article}
```

**LLM Response**

```text
Key points of the article:
- Company X announced the release of a new product Y.
- The product will be available from December 1st.
- Key features of the product include A, B, and C.
- The product is expected to change the market thanks to innovative technologies.
```

### Decision Making (Task Completed or Not)

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a quality control expert. Your task is to assess whether a task has been completed based on the criteria provided.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Task: Write an article about the impact of technology on education.
Criteria:
1. Minimum 1000 words.
2. Include real-life examples.
3. Mention recent research.
```

**LLM Response**

```text
Task completed. The article contains 1050 words, includes real-life examples, and references recent research.
```

### Delegating to Other Agents

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a project manager. Your task is to determine which tasks can be delegated to other agents and propose a plan for their implementation.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Project: Mobile app development.
Tasks:
1. Interface design.
2. Functionality development.
3. Testing.
4. Promotion.
```

**LLM Response**

```text
1. Interface design - delegate to the designer.
2. Functionality development - delegate to the development team.
3. Testing - delegate to the QA department.
4. Promotion - delegate to the marketing team.
```

### Task Execution Critique, Error Detection

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a process improvement expert. Your task is to critically evaluate the execution of a task and identify possible errors or shortcomings.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Task: Prepare a quarterly sales report.
```

**LLM Response**

```text
The report has been prepared, but there is no analysis of sales trends. Seasonal fluctuations, which could give a more complete picture of the data, have also not been taken into account.
```
