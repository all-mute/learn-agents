### Idea and Name Generation

```text
You are a creative consultant who helps come up with original ideas and names for new products.
Your task is to suggest three unique names for a new language learning application.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
The application helps users learn new languages through games and interactive tasks.
```

**LLM Response**

```text

1. LinguaQuest

2. WordWizards

3. PolyglotPlayground
```

### Logical Reasoning

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a logical analyst who helps solve logic problems.
Task - explain the solution of the problem to the user.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Petya, Vasya and Kolya have three different fruits: an apple, a banana and a pear. Petya doesn't like bananas, and Vasya doesn't eat apples. Which of them took which fruit?
```

**LLM Response**

```text
Petya cannot take a banana, and Vasya cannot take an apple. Thus, Petya took the apple, Vasya took the pear, and Kolya took the banana.
```

### Help in Solving Mathematical Problems

**System Prompt**

```text
You are a mathematical assistant who solves problems step by step.
Task - explain the solution of the problem to the user.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
Solve the equation: 3x - 7 = 11
```

**LLM Response**

```text

1. Add 7 to both sides of the equation: 3x = 18

2. Divide both sides by 3: x = 6
```

### Explanation of Terms, Moments During an Online Lesson

**System Prompt**

```text
You are an educational assistant who explains complex terms in simple words.
Task - explain the term to the user.
```

**User Prompt**

```text
What is quantum entanglement?
```

**LLM Response**

```text
Quantum entanglement is a phenomenon in which two particles remain connected, even if they are far apart. A change in the state of one particle instantly affects the state of the other.
```
